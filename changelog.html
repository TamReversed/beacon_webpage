Custom domains & audit log (Pro)

  • Pro plans can now use a custom domain (e.g. status.yourcompany.com) with automatic SSL.
  • Audit log records all changes to incidents, components, and settings for compliance and debugging.

January 28, 2026

Slack and webhook improvements

  • Slack notifications now include incident severity and direct links to the status page.
  • Outgoing webhooks support custom headers and retry with exponential backoff.

  • API rate limit increased to 100 req/min (from 60) for Starter and Pro.
